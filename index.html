Energy & Water SETA

Welcome To The EWSETA Career Guidance Portal

A website dedicated to guiding the youth on various skills within SA’s Energy & Water industry
As one of South Africa’s 21 SETAs, the Energy and Water Sector Education and Training Authority
(EWSETA) was established in terms of Section 9 of the Skills Development Act (SDA), 97 of 1998,
as amended and reports to the Honourable Minister of Higher Education, Science and Technology
This portal aims to educate website visitors about the various careers in the Energy & Water sector
in South Africa.

Considering a career in the water & energy sectors? here at EWSETA We have just the right programmes for you,

Services.

Skills Planning

The role of skills development in addresing South Africa's triple challenge of poverty,inequality and unemployment requires a coordinated effort involving all relevant stakeholders,underpinned by quality reaseach that aims toinform the skills development planning for the sectors

Learning Programmes

learning programmes are funded by the EWSETA in accordance with skills development priorities as defined in the EWSETA Sector Skils Plan which is reviewed anually

Discretionary Grants

Discretionary Grants funding is Board approved and subject to strigent assesment of grant application received during an approved cretionary grant application window

Mandatory Grants

Employers in the energy and water sector who contribute to skills development levy and who have received approval for their annual WSP and ATR are eligible for Mandatory Grant Funding.

Our Mission
We are committed to working with stakeholders across the country, including government, businesses, and communities, to find solutions to the challenges facing our water and energy systems. By working together, we can ensure a brighter and more sustainable future for South Africa.
